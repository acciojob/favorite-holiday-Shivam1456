1. Haleakala National Park - A stunning national park with a massive volcanic crater, hiking trails, and incredible views.
  2. Road to Hana - A scenic drive along the eastern coast of Maui, with plenty of stops for waterfalls, beaches, and local food.
  3. Lahaina - A historic town with art galleries, boutiques, and museums. Don't miss the famous Banyan Tree!
  4. Makena Beach - A beautiful, secluded beach with crystal-clear waters and stunning sunsets.
  5. Whale Watching Tours - From December to May, take a whale watching tour and see these magnificent creatures up close.

Must-Try Foods

  • Poke - A Hawaiian dish made with raw fish and seasonings.
  • Shave Ice - A refreshing dessert made with shaved ice and flavored syrup.
  • Kalua Pork - A traditional Hawaiian dish made with slow-cooked pork.
  • Spam Musubi - A popular snack made with spam, rice, and seaweed.
  • Loco Moco - A hearty dish made with rice, a hamburger patty, a fried egg, and gravy.
